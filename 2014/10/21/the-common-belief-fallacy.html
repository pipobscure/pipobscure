The Common Belief Fallacy

A couple of centuries back people began to catch on to the fact that looking for disconfirming evidence was a better way to conduct research than proceeding from common belief.

Don’t you think it might be time to accept that “common beliefs” in a Judean carpenter’s son and his sky-daddy have enough disconfirming evidence to reject them?
